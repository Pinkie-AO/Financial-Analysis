# FINANCIAL ANALYSIS PERFORMANCE

Overview

This project presents an end-to-end financial performance analysis using Python and Power BI. The objective was to analyze sales, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and operational factors affecting business performance. 
The project follows the complete data analytics workflow—from data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA) in Python to interactive dashboard development in Power BI.

The dashboard provides management with actionable insights into revenue trends, profitability drivers, regional performance, customer segments, and operational efficiency to support data-driven decision-making.

Project Objectives

The project aimed to answer the following business questions:

1. How have revenue and profit changed over time?
   
2. Which products, product categories, customers, and regions contribute the most to revenue and profit?
   
3. Which operational factors affect profitability?
   
4. What strategic recommendations can improve business performance?


Key Insights

 Financial Performance
 
i)   The business generated $484,559.34 in total sales and $158,872.32 in profit between 2023 and 2025.

ii)  Revenue remained relatively stable over the three-year period, with a slight decline in 2024 followed by recovery in 2025.

iii) The company maintained a healthy overall profit margin of 32.79%.

 Product Performance
 
i)   Furniture generated the highest revenue and total profit, contributing over half of total sales.

ii)  Clothing & Accessories achieved the highest profit margin (37.64%), making it the most profitable category relative to sales.

iii) Office Supplies generated the lowest revenue and recorded the majority of loss-making transactions.

 Regional Performance
 
i)   Europe and North America were the highest-performing regions in both revenue and profit.

ii)  Asia Pacific also made a significant contribution.

iii) South America and Middle East & Africa generated comparatively lower sales and profits.
 
 Customer Performance
 
i)  The Consumer segment accounted for the largest share of total revenue.

ii) Revenue was distributed across many customers, indicating low dependency on a small number of high-value clients.
 
 Operational Performance
 
i)  Profit margins consistently decreased as discount percentages increased.

ii) Credit Card payments generated the highest revenue and profit.

iii)Larger order quantities generally resulted in higher average profits.

iv) A total of 272 loss-making transactions were identified, with Office Supplies accounting for the majority.

Recommendations

a. Review discount strategies to reduce unnecessary margin erosion while maintaining competitive pricing.

b. Continue investing in high-performing product categories such as Furniture while expanding Clothing & Accessories, which delivers the highest profit margins.

c. Investigate the Office Supplies category to identify pricing, procurement, or operational issues contributing to frequent losses.

d. Optimize shipping operations in regions with higher logistics costs to improve overall profitability.

e. Focus customer retention and marketing efforts on the Consumer segment while identifying opportunities to grow the Corporate and Home Office segments.

f. Encourage larger order quantities through carefully designed promotions that maintain healthy profit margins.

g. Monitor key financial KPIs regularly through the Power BI dashboard to support data-driven strategic decisions.
